## Welcome Business Owners

BeanQuick empowers local coffee shops and cafes to reach more customers online. This guide covers everything you need to know to successfully operate your business on the platform.

---

## Getting Started

### Applying as a Business

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit Application">
    Navigate to the business registration page and provide:
    - Business name
    - Email address
    - NIT (Tax ID)
    - Phone number
    - Physical address
    - Business description
    - Logo image
    - Photo of your establishment
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Review">
    Your application will be reviewed by BeanQuick administrators. This typically takes 1-3 business days.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive Activation Email">
    Once approved, you'll receive an email with an activation link.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Activate Your Account">
    Click the activation link and set your password to complete setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Make sure to use high-quality images for your logo and establishment photo - these are the first things customers see!
</Tip>

### Account Activation Process

After administrator approval:
1. Check your email for the activation link
2. Click the link (it contains a unique token)
3. Validate the token on the activation page
4. Set your secure password
5. Log in with your email and new password

<Warning>
Activation links expire after a certain period. If your link expires, contact support for a new one.
</Warning>

## Business Profile Setup

### Updating Your Profile

Access **"Perfil"** (Profile) from your business dashboard to update:

- Business name
- Description
- Contact information (phone, address)
- Logo
- Establishment photo
- Operating hours

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Profile">
    Click on **"Perfil"** in the business navigation menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Information">
    Update any fields you want to change.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **"Update"** to save your modifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Open/Closed Status

Control when customers can place orders:

<Card title="Store Status Toggle" icon="toggle">
  From your dashboard, use the **"Abrir tienda"** / **"Cerrar tienda"** button to:
  - **Open**: Customers can browse and place orders
  - **Closed**: Your store appears as closed; no new orders can be placed
</Card>

<Tip>
Close your store during off-hours or when you can't fulfill orders. Customers will see a "Cerrada" indicator on your business card.
</Tip>

## Product Management

### Adding Products

Navigate to **"Mis Productos"** (My Products) to manage your inventory.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Product">
    Access the product management page and click the add button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Product Details">
    Provide:
    - Product name
    - Description
    - Category (select from available categories)
    - Price
    - Stock quantity
    - Product image
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Product">
    Click **"Guardar"** (Save) to add the product to your catalog.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Editing Products

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Product">
    Locate the product in your product list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    Click the edit icon (pencil) next to the product.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Details">
    Modify any fields: name, description, category, price, stock, or image.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click save to update the product.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Stock

The product table displays stock levels with color-coded indicators:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="In Stock" icon="check">
    **Green badge**: Stock above 10 units
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Low Stock" icon="exclamation">
    **Orange badge**: Stock between 1-10 units (warning icon)
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Out of Stock" icon="times">
    **Red badge**: 0 units ("AGOTADO")
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
Products with 0 stock cannot be ordered by customers. Update stock regularly to avoid missing sales.
</Warning>

### Deleting Products

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Product">
    Find the product you want to remove.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Delete">
    Click the delete icon (trash can).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Confirm when prompted. This action cannot be undone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Order Management

### Viewing Incoming Orders

Access **"Gestión de Pedidos"** (Order Management) to see all customer orders.

Orders are organized by status with real-time updates every 30 seconds.

### Order Statuses and Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pagado (Paid)">
    Customer has paid. Order is waiting for you to start preparation.
    
    **Action**: Click **"Empezar Cocina"** (Start Kitchen) when ready to begin.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Preparando (Preparing)">
    Order is being prepared.
    
    **Action**: Click **"Marcar como Listo"** (Mark as Ready) when complete.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Listo (Ready)">
    Order is ready for customer pickup.
    
    **Action**: Click **"Confirmar Entrega"** (Confirm Delivery) when customer picks up.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Entregado (Delivered)">
    Order completed successfully. No further action needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Order Details

Click on any order to expand and view:
- Customer name
- Pickup time
- Individual products with quantities and images
- Total order value

### Canceling Orders

<Warning>
Use cancellation sparingly - it negatively impacts customer experience.
</Warning>

You can cancel orders in **Pagado**, **Preparando**, or **Listo** status:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Order">
    Locate the order you need to cancel.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Cancelar">
    Click the **"Cancelar Pedido"** (Cancel Order) button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Confirm the cancellation when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Filtering Orders

Use the filter tabs to view orders by status:
- **Pagado**: New orders to prepare
- **Preparando**: Currently in kitchen
- **Listo**: Ready for pickup
- **Entregado**: Completed orders
- **Cancelado**: Canceled orders

Each tab shows the count of orders in that status.

## Downloading Reports

### PDF Reports

Generate comprehensive business reports:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Time Period">
    Choose the period you want to report on (Day/Week/Month/Year).
  </Step>

- Sales summary
- Performance metrics
- Top products
- Sales charts
- Customer feedback statistics

<Tip>
Download monthly reports for accounting and business analysis purposes.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Q: Why can't customers see my store?**
- Check that your store status is set to "Open"
- Verify your business profile is complete
- Ensure you have products with available stock

**Q: How do I handle incorrect orders?**
- Contact the customer directly using their contact information
- Use the cancel function only as a last resort

**Q: My dashboard isn't updating**
- Refresh your browser
- Check your internet connection
- The dashboard auto-refreshes every 30 seconds for orders
