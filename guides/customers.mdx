## Welcome to BeanQuick

BeanQuick is your gateway to discovering and ordering from local coffee shops and cafes. This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of your experience.

---

## Getting Started

### Registration and Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Navigate to the registration page and provide:
    - Full name
    - Email address
    - Password (must be secure)
    
    Click **Register** to create your account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Log In">
    Use your email and password to access the platform. Your authentication token will be stored securely for future sessions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Make sure to use a valid email address - you'll need it for order confirmations and updates.
</Tip>

---

## Browsing Businesses

### Discovering Coffee Shops

Once logged in, you'll see the **Customer Dashboard** featuring:

- **Hero Section**: Welcome banner with search functionality
- **Business Cards**: Grid display of all available cafes
- **Real-time Status**: See which businesses are currently open or closed

<Card title="Business Information" icon="store">
  Each business card displays:
  - Business name and logo
  - Description
  - Average rating (out of 5 stars)
  - Current status (Open/Closed)
  - "View Menu" button
</Card>

### Searching for Businesses

Use the search bar in the hero section to filter businesses by name. The results update instantly as you type.

### Viewing a Business Menu

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Business">
    Click on any business card or the "View Menu" button to view their products.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Products">
    View the full catalog of available products with:
    - Product images
    - Names and descriptions
    - Prices
    - Stock availability
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
You can only place orders from businesses that are currently **Open**. Closed businesses will display a "Cerrada" (Closed) overlay.
</Warning>

## Shopping Cart and Checkout

### Adding Items to Cart

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Products">
    Browse the business menu and click "Add to Cart" on desired items.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjust Quantities">
    - Use the cart interface to increase or decrease quantities
    - Remove items you no longer want
    - View running total in real-time
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Cart">
    Before checkout, verify:
    - Product names and quantities
    - Individual prices
    - Total amount
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Placing an Order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Proceed to Checkout">
    Click the checkout button when ready to complete your order.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Pickup Time">
    Choose your preferred pickup time from available slots.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Order">
    Review all details and click **"Confirmar y Pagar"** (Confirm and Pay).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Process payment through the integrated Mercado Pago gateway. Once payment is confirmed, your order will be sent to the business.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Orders are only sent to the business after successful payment. You'll receive confirmation once the payment is processed.
</Tip>

## Managing Your Orders

### Viewing Order History

Access **"Mis Pedidos"** (My Orders) to see all your past and current orders.

#### Order Statuses

<Card title="Order Lifecycle" icon="clock">
  - **Pagado** (Paid): Payment confirmed, awaiting preparation
  - **Preparando** (Preparing): Business is making your order
  - **Listo** (Ready): Order is ready for pickup
  - **Entregado** (Delivered): Order completed
  - **Cancelado** (Canceled): Order was canceled
</Card>

### Filtering Orders

Use the filter menu at the top to view orders by status:
- All orders
- Pending
- Preparing
- Ready
- Delivered
- Canceled

### Order Details

Click on any order to expand and view:
- Business name and logo
- Business address
- Pickup time
- Individual products with images and quantities
- Total amount
- Current status

### Canceling Orders

<Warning>
You can only cancel orders with **"Pendiente"** (Pending) status. Once a business starts preparing your order, cancellation is no longer available.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Order Details">
    Find the order you want to cancel in your order list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Cancel">
    Click the **"Cancelar Pedido"** button (only visible for pending orders).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Confirm the cancellation when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Ratings and Reviews

### Leaving a Review

Once an order is marked as **"Entregado"** (Delivered), you can rate the products.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Delivered Order">
    Navigate to **Mis Pedidos** and find a delivered order.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Calificar">
    Click the **"Calificar"** (Rate) button next to any product in the order.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide Rating">
    - Select 1-5 stars
    - Add an optional written comment
    - Click **"Enviar Calificación"** (Submit Rating)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Your reviews help other customers make informed decisions and help businesses improve their service!
</Tip>

### Managing Your Reviews

Access **"Mis Calificaciones"** (My Reviews) to:
- View all your past reviews
- See product details and business names
- Edit existing reviews (change rating or comment)
- Delete reviews you no longer want to keep

#### Editing a Review

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Review">
    Locate the review in your reviews list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    Click the **"Editar"** (Edit) button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Details">
    Modify the star rating or comment as desired.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **"Guardar cambios"** (Save changes).
  </Step>
</Steps>
